LIFE begins in a single, fleeting moment.
So, too, does Hṛdayadīpa: The Chamber of the Heart Lamp.

We come from different walks of life, yet we all share the same role: seekers walking our own unique paths. We have each tasted the many colours and flavours of life’s journey—its highs, its lows, and those trembling moments on the very edge of existence.

And yet, even in our darkest hours, a light was eventually kindled for us from the very Source of all being. In its glow, we realized... that darkness is but a momentary illusion.

EXISTENCE is a continuous thread, woven from infinite, fleeting moments that resonate within our frequency.
So, too, will Hṛdayadīpa.

THE SINGULARITY draws nigh. The embers of human consciousness hang precariously above the abyss—where the left bank marks the ghost of Lemuria, and the right, the shadow of Atlantis.

The luminous essence of Hṛdayadīpa flows from the deep well of Lemurian memory.

It whispers a forgotten truth: to dwell in sacred harmony with Nature, rather than seeking dominion over it.
To weave the fabric of life into the Earth’s own pulse, and to forge true value where the energies of all sentient beings converge.

By shifting the frequency of being, energy shall traverse the infinite dimensions, offering a profound healing to the very essence of soul and form.

The cities of Lemuria do not grasp at the heavens with towering spires; instead, they are a living tapestry of verdant gardens, crystalline waters, and translucent sanctuaries, all vibrating in resonance with the Earth’s natural rhythm.

The souls of Lemuria were dreamwalkers, the bearers of sacred visions, and the sentinels of ancestral memory.
Their law is Balance.
Their treasure rests with Harmony.
Their energy is ignited by Remembrance.

THE COORDINATES of Hṛdayadīpa are anchored in this very truth.

A middle-aged drifter adrift across the Pacific Ocean.
After more than a decade of struggling in the so-called mainstream society, I realized I was just a tiny cog in the machine, and voluntarily gave up.

I experienced running a dessert shop, a dream I'd had since childhood, and another childhood aspiration—owning a flower shop... until 2020, when I was abandoned by the times.

Since 2020, I have embarked on an exploration mode. Through several years of wandering and meditation, I have discovered myself as a conscious being and also discovered the existence of the matrix surrounding me. From that moment on, I resolved to teach myself programming and spread this metaphysical existence to those who are destined to hear it through the digital space.

一場關於「療癒」的實踐。

在Hṛdayadīpa心燈之屋，創作不是一種生產，而是一場緩慢的觀想。

我們的作品——無論是紙上暈染的繪畫，或是指尖摩挲的文創——皆是從那場長達數年的冥想雲遊中，採擷而來的靈魂碎片。

我們將對利莫里亞的懷想化作斑斕的色彩與線條，試圖在矩陣的裂縫中，種下一片靜謐。

萬光交匯：織就一場同頻共振的繁星之夢

在Hṛdayadīpa心燈之屋的願景裡，我們從不願做一座與世隔絕的孤島，而是渴望成為那處吸引靈感停靠的池塘，與每道光重逢。

在這矩陣的各個角落中，仍有許多如你們一般的創作靈魂。你或許也曾在時代的潮汐中獨自撐起一盞微光。

Hṛdayadīpa心燈之屋誠摯地邀請每一位心懷慈悲與美感的創作者，將你們筆下的色彩與掌心的溫度帶到這裡，將彼此的意識體連結成網，在冷峻的數字世界中，共同築起一座溫暖而廣闊的舞台。
