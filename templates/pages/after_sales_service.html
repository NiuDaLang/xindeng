售後保障與退換貨政策

最後更新日期：2026年6月

一、 30天安心退換貨保障

自您收到實體商品之次日起算（依據快遞簽收日期為準），新燈藝術商店提供 30 天的退換貨申請窗口。

※ 提醒您：提出申請前，請務必先透過官方電子信箱或後台客服與我們聯繫，切勿自行寄回包裹。

二、 適用商品與完好狀態標準

退換貨服務僅適用於實體商品，且必須符合以下完整度標準：

  • 原創畫作與藝術品： 必須保持出廠時的原始狀態。無污損、無刮傷、未經裝框改造、防撕標籤未受破壞，且所有隨附的藝術家收藏證書（C.O.A.）均須一併退回。
  • 文具、手工藝品與家居摆飾： 包裝必須未經拆封。內外包裝完整、零配件無缺失，不影響二次銷售。

❌ 排除條款（不適用退換貨）：數位電子商品 (E-Products)、電子授權碼、虛擬現金券 (Vouchers) 以及任何客製化定製藝術品，一經售出或發送即無法辦理退換貨。

三、 物流費用責任歸屬 (行業標準)

依據跨境電商國際慣例，退換貨之運費劃分如下：

  • 買家承擔（個人因素）： 若因「不喜歡、與想像不符、買錯尺寸或款式」等個人審美與主觀原因申請退貨，雙向往返運費、包裝成本及可能產生的海關進口關稅將由買家全額承擔。
  • 商家承擔（瑕疵與品質因素）： 若商品在長途運輸中發生嚴重受損、破裂、原畫嚴重受潮，或團隊漏發、寄錯品項，由此產生的退換貨國際運費及清關規費將由新燈藝術商店全額承擔。

四、 運輸受損 (Damaged Art) 處理流程

藝術品十分脆弱。若您在收到包裹時發現包裝嚴重破損、變形，請依循以下步驟以保障您的權益：

  1. 請在簽收包裹時，當場拍照或錄影留存外部紙箱破損外觀。
  2. 開箱時請錄影，清晰拍攝商品受損的具體部位，並於 48 小時內將影片與訂單編號發送至客服信箱。
  3. 經團隊審查確認後，我們將立即為您安排全額退款或啟動藝術品修復、重新補發程序。

After-Sales & Returns Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. 30-Day Evaluation Window

XinDeng Art Shop provides a 30-day return or exchange eligibility window, commencing the calendar day immediately following tracking delivery confirmation.

*Note: Please register an inquiry via your Personal Registry or support email before shipping items back; unsolicited packages cannot be processed.

2. Product Eligibility & Conditions

Returns are **strictly limited to physical assets** adhering to global gallery standards:

  • Original Masterpieces & Artworks: Must arrive in pristine condition. Free of scuffs, scratches, framing modifications, or seal damage. All attached Certificate of Authenticity (C.O.A.) documents must be returned inside the container.
  • Stationery, Crafts & Decor: Manufactured packaging must remain unopened, factory-sealed, and completely undamaged to qualify for resale layout requirements.

❌ Non-Returnable Items: Digital Assets (E-products), downloadable graphic licenses, virtual Cash Vouchers, and customized bespoke commissions cannot be cancelled or returned under any circumstances once processed.

3. Shipping Cost Allocations (Market Standard)

Fulfillment infrastructure cost assignments mirror cross-border e-commerce criteria:

  • Buyer Responsibility (Change of Mind): If an exchange or return is initiated due to personal aesthetic variations, sizing miscalculations, or subjective criteria, all multi-directional international logistics fees, return custom entries, and packaging overhead remain the exclusive financial responsibility of the buyer.
  • Seller Responsibility (Defect or Damaged Arrival): If a piece exhibits structurally verified damage, structural faults, wrong item dispatches, or moisture disruption sustained mid-transit, all alternative transit, clearance fees, and return billing will be covered entirely by XinDeng Art Shop.

4. Damaged Art Claim Framework

Fine art requires extreme care. Should your parcel arrive visibly compromised, please follow this legal protection blueprint:

  1. Document the external shipping container’s condition via high-resolution photography or video footage immediately upon arrival.
  2. Record an uninterrupted unboxing sequence illustrating the precise visual location of the damage. Forward this evidence to support within 48 hours alongside your order serial key.
  3. Upon confirmation, we will swiftly initiate a comprehensive financial credit refund or proceed with an expedited reproduction/restoration process.
