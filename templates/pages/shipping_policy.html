Shipping Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Shipping Origins & Destinations

All physical manifestations are meticulously packaged and dispatched directly from Mainland China.

We currently provide premium logistics fulfillment to the following countries and regions: Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan, South Korea, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Australia, and New Zealand.

2. Artwork, Crafts & Stationary Packaging

We respect the fragile essence of fine art paintings, interior decor, and intricate crafts. Every original masterpiece or fragile object is outfitted with specialized moisture-proof, shock-absorbent, and crush-resistant materials designed for international transit.

Stationery, dynamic prints, and fine paper goods are encased within branded protective layouts, ensuring they arrive in pristine, gallery-ready condition.

3. Order Processing & Tracking

Standard shipments are fulfilled within 3 to 5 business days following payment confirmation (excluding bespoke commissions or pre-order pieces, which follow specialized lead times detailed on their respective product pages).

Once dispatched, an automated notification containing a dedicated Tracking Number will be forwarded to your registered email directory. Real-time updates can be referenced inside your Personal Registry.

4. Customs, Duties & Import Tariffs

Cross-border shipments may trigger import taxes, customs clearances, or local handling fees levied by the destination territory. All associated administrative fees remain the exclusive responsibility of the buyer (recipient). Please contact your local municipal customs office for clear calculation matrices.

5. Digital Goods & Virtual Fulfillment (E-Products)

For instant licenses, digital assets, or digital collector items, paths are granted instantly or shared via automated email dispatches upon successful checkout validation.

⚠️ Notice: Should you encounter network restrictions, corrupted files, or delivery failures concerning an e-product purchase, please communicate with us directly. We will resolve the disruption and supply alternative direct transfers manually.

配送須知

最後更新日期：2026年6月

一、 配送範圍與出貨地點

所有實體商品均自中國大陸精心包裝並直接寄出。

目前提供穩定配送服務之國家與地區（不含中國大陸地區）包含：香港、澳門、台灣、韓國、日本、新加坡、馬西亞、澳洲、紐西蘭。

二、 實體藝術品與文創商品包裝

我們深知藝術品與精緻工藝品的脆弱與珍貴。為了確保原畫作、手工藝品及家居擺飾在長途運輸中完好無損，團隊均採用高規格的專業防震、防水、防壓包裝。

紙質文具、手帳用品等輕量物件，亦會伴隨定製紙盒或氣泡保護層寄出，力求優雅與安全兼備。

三、 配送時效與追蹤

商品在訂單確認且款項支付成功後，一般於 3 至 5 個工作天內安排出貨（訂製或預售藝術品除外，具體時間以商品頁面標示為準）。

包裹寄出後，系統將自動向您註冊的電子信箱發送包裹追蹤碼 (Tracking Number)。您可以隨時登入會員中心或至指定快遞官網追蹤物流動態。

四、 關稅與進口規費

跨境運輸可能涉及目的地國家或地區所徵收之關稅、增值稅 (VAT) 或其他清關手續費。此類規費依法需由買家（收件人）自行承擔。各國海關政策不同，具體徵稅標準請諮詢您當地的海關機構。

五、 數位電子商品 (E-Products) 說明

若您購買的是數位授權、電子素材或壁紙等非實體商品，系統會在款項確認後自動開放下載鏈接或將檔案發送至您的電子信箱。

⚠️ 提示：若您在下載、解壓或接收數位商品時遇到任何技術阻礙，請隨時聯絡我們的客服團隊，我們將竭誠為您人工解決並補發檔案。
