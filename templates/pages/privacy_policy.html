Privacy & Data Security Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information Collected

To complete transactions, register profiles, and optimize user interaction across XinDeng Art Shop, we gather specific user registry points:

  • Identity Essentials: Your name, registered electronic mail address, profile password markers, and contact nodes.
  • Account Passwords: Your access pins are stored using high-grade, irreversible cryptographic hashing algorithms. Our server architecture and site administrators can never view or read your raw, plaintext password.
  • Physical Logistics Elements: Shipping addresses, recipient identities, and required cross-border municipal declaration paperwork.

2. Purpose of Processing Your Data

Your information remains secure. Collected nodes are deployed solely under standard market criteria to facilitate operational functions:

  • Fulfillment and Logistics: Sharing address coordinates with international freight couriers to verify the transport of original paintings, stationery, or home decor.
  • Direct Studio Marketing: Distributing internal exhibition notices, promo codes, or gallery news related exclusively to our shop (with full opt-out features available at any time).
  • E-Product Deliveries: Validating identity layers to dispatch secured download paths for virtual collector assets directly to your inbox.

3. Cookie Execution and Future Trajectory

Our server framework does not actively use tracking cookies or persistent analytic pixels to log user behavior profiles.

*Future Optimization Notice: To improve page loading speeds, save your default currency selection, and optimize Three.js render settings, we may introduce baseline tracking cookies later. A standard configuration prompt will be added to let you manage your tracking consent.*

4. Payment Security & Future Credit Card Integration

Currently, all digital checkouts are managed via secure PayPal redirect overlays. **Our application server never intercepts, accesses, or records your credit card strings or private transaction pin codes.**

Future Integration Roadmap: Should we implement direct, on-site credit card fields in a future release, we commit to deploying industry-standard PCI-DSS architecture, full SSL tunneling, and bank-level end-to-end data encryption to safeguard your payment processing details.

個人資料保護政策

最後更新日期：2026年6月

一、 我們收集的個人資料

當您在「新燈藝術商店」（XinDeng Art Shop）註冊、瀏覽或購買商品時，我們將依據業務實際需要收集以下資訊：

  • 基本帳戶與聯絡資訊： 包括您的姓名、電子信箱、註冊密碼及聯絡電話。
  • 註冊密碼： 您的登入密碼經由高強度單向雜湊演算法加密後進行儲存，本站伺服器及任何管理人員皆「絕對無法」讀取您的明文密碼。
  • 實體物流資訊： 專用於實體包裹投遞的收件地址、收件人姓名及必要的報關聯絡資訊。

二、 資料使用目的

我們承諾收集的個人資料僅用於以下合理且必要的合規範圍，絕不外洩、轉售或向無關第三方披露：

  • 商品配送與訂單履行： 將收件資訊提供給國際物流承運商，以確保畫作、手工藝品或文具安全送達。
  • 自有產品營銷與通知： 僅用於向您發送本商店的最新作品特展通知、專屬折扣碼或會員促銷簡報（您可隨時選取取消訂閱）。
  • 數位商品發送： 驗證您的身分並將電子非實體商品的下載鏈接安全寄送至您的電子信箱。

三、 Cookie 技術與未來規劃說明

目前本站後台技術架構尚未正式啟用任何用於追蹤、使用者畫像或第三方廣告投放的 Cookie 紀錄。

未來升級展望：為了優化「Silk」主題的網頁流暢度、記住您的幣種選取偏好以及分析 3D 煙火場景的渲染效能，本站未來可能會啟用基礎 Cookie。屆時我們將主動彈出合規提示，保障您的知情權與自由選取權。

四、 支付數據安全與未來信用卡升級規劃

當前本站線上即時結算完全透過第三方平台（PayPal）進行。在該模式下，您的所有銀行卡敏感數據均在 PayPal 安全加密的彈窗內處理，本站伺服器完全不會、亦無權接觸或儲存您的任何信用卡密碼與敏感交易數據。

未來升級展望：若未來本站開放直接於頁面輸入信用卡進行收單，我們承諾將全面引入最高規格的國際金融級 PCI-DSS 安全認證與 SSL 全段高強度傳輸加密，進一步升級您的隱私數據屏障。
