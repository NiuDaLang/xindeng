Intellectual Property Protection

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Protection of Our Original Creations

All original paintings, digital wallpaper assets, craftsmanship designs, user interface code bases, trademarks, and multimedia files displayed across XinDeng Art Shop are legally protected under international copyright and intellectual property frameworks:

  • Physical Ownership vs. Copyright: Acquisition of an original physical masterpiece grants the collector ownership of the tangible object and private exhibition rights only. Commercial reproduction, digitization, retail merchandising, or promotional scaling without explicit written consent is strictly prohibited.
  • Digital Goods Scope: Digital allocations (E-Products) are licensed strictly for personal, non-commercial use. Sharing, distributing on public cloud servers, reselling, or embedding files into alternative profitable assets is forbidden.

2. Adherence to Third-Party Rights

We respect and advocate for the protection of third-party intellectual property. All computational engines (e.g., Three.js frameworks), typography fonts, or auxiliary visual packages implemented across this environment are deployed in strict compliance with open-source licensing guidelines (such as MIT or Apache) and signed partner agreements.

We are firmly committed to preventing the introduction, modification, or sale of any unauthorized replicas or pirated visual artifacts within our storefront matrix.

3. Notice & Takedown Protocol

Should you identify any content or artisan merchandise within our domain that potentially infringes upon your registered IP rights, please submit a written dispute notice to our secure digital support desk incorporating:

  1. Verifiable proof of intellectual property ownership or authorized representation documentation.
  2. Direct URL pathways or screenshots pinpointing the disputed asset alongside links to the original work.

*Resolution Guarantee: Upon processing a verified and actionable notice, our administration will complete an audit within 48 hours and swiftly quarantine or remove any validated infringing elements.*

智慧財產權保護條款

最後更新日期：2026年6月

一、 本站原創權益保護 (Our Intellectual Property)

新燈藝術商店（XinDeng Art Shop）平台內展示的所有原創實體畫作、數位壁紙、工藝設計、網頁視覺代碼、品牌商標及多媒體影像，其智慧財產權均受國際法律保護：

  • 實體畫作所有權 vs. 著作權： 購買原創實體畫作僅代表您獲得該實體作品的所有權與私人收藏展示權。未經書面明確授權，買家不得將畫作進行商業複製、數位化二次轉售、印製商品或用於任何營利性廣告。
  • 數位商品使用界限： 購買本站數位電子商品（E-Products），僅代表獲得個人非商業用途授權。嚴禁將檔案上傳至公共網路、轉售或融入他人商業創作中。

二、 尊重並保護他人權益 (Respecting Others' IP)

我們極其珍視並嚴格遵守對第三方智慧財產權的保護。本站所有引用的開源技術組件（如 Three.js 底層引擎）、字體、開源庫或合作藝術家素材，均嚴格遵循對應的授權協議（如 MIT, Apache 或 藝術家商業合作契約）。

我們承諾：絕不故意上架、兜售、複製或改編任何涉嫌侵權、仿冒或未經第三方合法授權的圖像或工藝創意。

三、 侵權投訴處理機制 (Notice and Takedown)

若您發現本站任何頁面內容、文創商品或展示物涉嫌侵犯了您的合法智慧財產權，請立即提供包含以下資訊的書面通知，並發送至我們的官方信箱：

  1. 您的合法權利人身分證明或合法授權委託書。
  2. 涉嫌侵權之本站商品網址、截圖與被侵權之原創作品對比鏈接。

    3. ⚠️ 承諾：我們在收到完整且有效的合法通知後，將於 48 小時內啟動人工審查。若侵權屬實，將立即對相關商品或內容進行下架隔離處置。
